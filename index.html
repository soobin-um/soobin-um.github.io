About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI, KAIST, advised by Prof. Jong Chul Ye. My research focuses on generative modeling, with a special interest in robust and inclusive AI. I also explore the intersection of generative models and physics, particularly in photonic structures. I previously worked as a senior researcher at Agency for Defense Development (ADD) for 7.5 years, specializing in wireless communication systems and defense applications.
