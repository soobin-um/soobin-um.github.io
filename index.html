Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Graduate School of AI, KAIST, advised by Prof. Jong Chul Ye. My research focuses on generative modeling, with a special interest in fair and inclusive AI, particularly for underrepresented classes in vision datasets. I previously worked as a senior researcher at the Agency for Defense Development (ADD) for 7.5 years, specializing in wireless communication systems and defense applications.

Education:
- Ph.D. in AI, KAIST (2021–present)
- M.S. in EE, KAIST
- B.S. in Media Communication Engineering, Hanyang University (Summa Cum Laude)

Research Interests:
Generative Models · Fairness in AI · Diffusion Models · Minority Representation · Scientific Design via AI
